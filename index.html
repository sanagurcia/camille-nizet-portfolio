about

I'm a work-in-progress art director on a cliché mission to save the world.

It all started at the age 10 when I stumbled on a brochure to save the dolphins. As much as I loved dolphins, I just couldn't get past the fact the whole thing was written in comic sans with neon on neon.

I decided then and there in my 10 year old crooked-tooth-glory that good causes deserved better. Good causes deserve good design.

Since then I’ve had braces and a bachelor’s in social sciences so I can understand the world a bit better before I try to change it. I was born in Belgium and grew up in the US, but migrated back in search of better beer and some joie de vivre. I’ve moved around quite a bit: from Maastricht to Brighton to Brussels to Berlin, trying to find the gaps I can help fill. I like coffee, bees, and a sense of purpose, but I avoid running at all costs.

I'm a third-culture kid, through + through

I love bees. I grew up with them always buzzing around since my mom was a beekeeper, so here I am trying to share my love with the world. Everyone knows we need them, but I also want to show just how cool they really are.

Reimagine your favorite movie. So I did. Finally, I found a purpose for my useless skill of memorizing movie lines. A modern twist on Monty Python and The Holy Grail. In my opinion, a movie that should be watched at least twice. A year.

How do we get gen z to make a change towards a more sustainable lifestyle? By focusing a smaller part of climate change, water consumption, we created an integrated campaign to target gen z and convince them to take shorter showers.
